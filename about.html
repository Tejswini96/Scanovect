About Us

Welcome to Scanovect

At Scanovect, we bring real-world objects into the digital realm and transform your ideas into precision-manufactured reality. As a leading provider of 3D Scanning and 3D Printing Services, we empower innovators, engineers, designers, and manufacturers with smart, reliable, and high-quality digital solutions — tailored for industries ranging from aerospace and automotive to healthcare, architecture, and education.

At Scanovect, we are redefining the future of digital manufacturing through advanced 3D Scanning and 3D Printing solutions. We help businesses transform physical objects into highly accurate digital models and convert innovative ideas into precision-engineered products with speed, accuracy, and reliability.

Driven by innovation and powered by cutting-edge technology, we deliver end-to-end solutions for reverse engineering, rapid prototyping, product development, dimensional inspection, custom manufacturing, and digital archiving. Every project is executed with a strong focus on quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Our expertise serves a diverse range of industries, including aerospace, automotive, healthcare, architecture, industrial manufacturing, education, and consumer product design. Whether you're developing a new product, improving an existing design, or preserving complex geometries, our team provides solutions that reduce development time, optimize costs, and accelerate innovation.

At Scanovect, we believe precision drives progress. Our mission is to empower businesses, engineers, designers, and innovators with world-class digital engineering solutions that transform ideas into reality. How It Works

From Object to Output in 5 Steps

01

Consultation

Share your project requirements, part complexity, and desired output format.

02

Scanning

We capture your object using the appropriate scanner technology for your geometry.

03

Processing

Raw scan data is cleaned, aligned, and converted into a usable 3D model.

04

CAD / Print

Model is converted to CAD or sent directly for 3D printing based on your needs.

05

Delivery

Final files or physical prints delivered with a full inspection report.
